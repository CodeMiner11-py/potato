Why are potatoes healthy?

Potatoes are a nutrient-packed food that offers several health benefits when consumed in moderation. Rich in vitamins like C and B6, along with essential minerals such as potassium, they contribute to immune support, brain health, and proper cell function. Potatoes are also a good source of fiber, particularly when eaten with the skin, which aids in digestion and promotes gut health. Their antioxidants help protect against oxidative stress, potentially lowering the risk of chronic diseases like cancer and heart disease.
