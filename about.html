About The Creators

About Eshaan

Eshaan is a young creator with a passion for technology and space. He began his journey at just 9 years old by launching the Kids Learning Lab podcast, where he has produced over 40 engaging episodes covering fun topics like the water cycle, Disney, and cheese. Eshaan has also published a mini-book and an e-guide titled “How I Make This Podcast – Digital Guide,” sharing his insights and experiences with podcasters.

About Ayan

Ayan is a curious and creative young kid with a passion for coding, especially in HTML. He loves building his own websites and experimenting with different designs to make them look cool and interactive. Always eager to learn, Ayan dreams of becoming a web developer one day and sharing his creations with the world!
